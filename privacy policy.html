Privacy Policy for Cookie Jar

Effective date: 25th july 2024

Introduction

Cookie Jar is a Chrome extension designed to help users view and manage cookies on their current webpage.

Data Collection

We collect cookie data from the current webpage to display information about cookies to the user. This data is only accessed when the user activates the extension to view the cookies on the page.

Usage of Data

The collected cookie data is used solely for displaying details about the cookies on the current page. We do not use the data for any other purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not share collected data with third parties. The data is used exclusively within the extension for the purpose of displaying cookie information to the user.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect collected data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

User Rights

Users can request access to or deletion of their data by contacting us at niharikapremkumar@gmail.com.

Contact Information
